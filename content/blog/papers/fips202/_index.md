In this series we're laymanizing NIST's latest Secure Hashing Algorithm (a.k.a "SHA") standard.
Let's talk about what it is, how it works, and why it matters (a lot!) in your daily life.

<!--more-->

NIST publishes the standard as FIPS 202. You can access it directly from NIST
[here](https://nvlpubs.nist.gov/nistpubs/fips/nist.fips.202.pdf). It's a pretty dry read. I think
that's government mandated or something.
